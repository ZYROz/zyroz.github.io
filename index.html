I am currently working as a Software Developer Engineer at Amazon.com, Inc. I graduated from Birla Institute of Technology and Science - Pilani, Goa Campus in 2015, with a master's degree in Mathematics and a bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

At Amazon, I am involoved in web development and associated back-end work for the Amazon Home Services team. Prior to joining Amazon, I have had the opportunity to work under Prof. Alexandre Gramfort at Telecom ParisTech, where drawing inspiration from the popular MNE-python tool, I developed a web based tool to analyze and interact with EEG/ MEG data obtained from brain signals. I have also been an intern at DreamWorks Animation, where I got to work closely with the studio's very talented FX artists.

My primary interests lie in the fields of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics and Human Computer Interaction.
